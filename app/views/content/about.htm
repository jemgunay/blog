Who am I?

Hey! I’m Jem, a software engineer living in central Manchester (UK) currently employed at bet365 as a Site Reliability Engineer (SRE, DevOps). Day to day, I primarily wield Golang to develop systems to reduce toil, monitor infrastructure and report on service health. In my previous role (Software Developer in the Systems Sports team), I wrote highly available and scalable Go microservices for driving content to the bet365 site with a strong emphasis on performance-optimising programming.


I’ve had the opportunity to delve into other exciting and modern technologies such as Apache Kafka, OpenTelemetry, Vue.js and InfluxDB to name a few, and I’m always curious about exploring new tech either at work or in my own time; I usually have several WIP personal software development projects on the go and my favourite completed ones will be showcased on this site. Outside of work, I also love attending music events, bouldering and dabbling with 3D printing. Cheers!
